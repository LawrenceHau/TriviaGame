This is a trivia game that will ask you questions based on the Mechanics of the game League of Legends

  • You will earn money and points for every question you get right

  • You will lose points for any question you get wrong

  • Points awarded are time based

  • Money awarded is a flat value

  • You can pay $100 for a hint

  • If you do not have $100, then you will only gain half of the points, and no money for answering the question right

  • If you answer the question wrong after using a hint you will lose double the points

  • You will not lose any points if you answer the question wrong, and spent money a hint
