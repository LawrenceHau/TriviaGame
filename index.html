This is a trivia game that will ask you questions based on the Mechanics of the game League of Legends

  • You have 15 seconds to answer questions, after those 15 seconds you will lose 1 score per second

  • You gain 5 points for every question you get right

  • You lose 5 point for every question you get wrong!

  • Press F11 for the full experience

  • Click start to begin
